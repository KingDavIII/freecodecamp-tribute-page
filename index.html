TIMELINE OF JASON GRACE'S LIFE:

  • 2003 - Born in Arizona,USA to Jupiter and Beryl Grace
  • 2005 - Ate a staple pin which gave him his lip scar
  • 2006 - Separated from mother and sister by stepmother Goddess Juno and was taken in by Mythical she-wolf Lupa
  • 2007 - Completed his training with Lupa
  • 2007 - Entered Camp Jupiter
  • 2007 - Rejected invitation to the first cohort and joined the fifth cohort to alter his destiny as jupiter's son
  • 2012 - Became Centurion of the fifth cohort
  • 2018 - Led tha assult on Mount Othrys where he fought through an army of Scythian Dracanae with the Roman demigods and defeated the Titain Krios himself in hand to hand combat during which he toppled the throne on Kronos
  • 2018 - Promoted to the rank of Praetor due to his victory against Krios
  • 2018 - Whisked away from Camp Jupiter without any memory of who he is
  • 2018 - Met Leo and Piper on the sghool bus he woke up in
  • 2018 - Arrives at Camp Half-Blood,takes up a mission,saves the Goddess Hera and reveals the whereabouts of Percy Jackson
  • 2018 - Became the head Counsellor of Cabin
  • 2019 - Joins the crew of Agro II,sails back to Camp Jupiter and starts a relationship with Piper McLean
  • 2019 - Steps down as Praetor and gives the title to Frank Zhang
  • 2019 - Teams up with the other members of the prophesy and the Gods to defeat Gaea
  • 2019 - Appointed as the Pontifex Maximus and moves to Camp Half-Blood permanently
  • 2019 - Searches for the remains of his best friend Leo Valdes who was lost in the war against Gaea with Piper
  • 2020 - Breaks up with Piper for unknown reasons
  • 2020 - Dies while fighting Emperor Caligula
  • 2020 - Buried with full honors
